Project Emerald

An online game about the philosophical implications of the coin toss

Background

Project Emerald is a statically hosted online game built from scratch with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. I submitted it as my final project for Writing 220, which had us transpose an existing essay into a different genre. During Project Emerald's development, I grasped the opportunity to take constructive peer criticism to evolve the game logic and behavior.

Status

The project's development was completed in late April 2019, after months of experimentation with other genres, including a video essay and an opinion editorial (op-ed).
