Revcoo.com

An online store for products sold by Revcoo

Background

Revcoo.com is a WordPress-based website served with an Amazon AWS EC2 server instance, via NGINX. The website uses the WooCommerce platform to display product listings, securely handle purchases, and store order information. The online store was launched on March 4th of 2019 with a short-term goal of simplifying direct sales and a long-term goal of moving away from third party selling platforms, which charge commission fees ranging from five to ten percent. As of August 2019, around 68 percent of total product revenue was collected via Revcoo.com. This share of direct sales continues to increase, which further enables lower prices overall.

Status

The website was developed over the course of several days in March 2019 and it has since been fully deployed. Updates to the website are made when necessary. Additionally, a custom bash script automatically backs up important files on the server every day.
